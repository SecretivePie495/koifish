Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Find quick, helpful answers to the most common questions about bookings, services, and what to expect at Sota Studio.

Book now
What are your hours of operation?

We’re open from Tuesday to Friday, 10 AM to 8 PM, and Saturday from 9 AM to 5 PM. We’re currently closed on Sunday and Monday.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes, we recommend booking in advance to ensure your preferred time and barber are available. Walk-ins are welcome when possible, but not guaranteed.

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of men’s grooming services including precision haircuts, beard trims, long/scissor cuts, and combo packages. We also provide custom sessions for more tailored needs.

Do you cut long or medium-length hair?

Absolutely. We specialize in long and scissor haircuts for men who want to maintain length with clean shaping and structure.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, and cash.

What’s your cancellation policy?

We kindly ask for at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a fee.
