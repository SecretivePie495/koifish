Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes — evenings and weekends book up fast. Booking ahead guarantees your slot.

What’s your cancellation policy?

Please give at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations/no-shows may be subject to a fee.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Credit/debit, Apple Pay, and cash.

Do you cut long or medium-length hair?

Absolutely — we do scissor work and longer styles with clean shaping and structure.
